## Design notes

### Leader-line marker redesign (v0.2.4 chart — leader-line markers step)
Source: Lao, GLD TradingView reference screenshot.
Current state: entry/exit markers render as large filled circles (createSeriesMarkers, position 'atPriceMiddle', size scaled by qty) sitting ON the candle at the fill price.
Target (per reference): SMALL precise dots at the exact fill price, each connected by a thin LEADER LINE to a price-tag label (e.g. "232.45" green for entry, "245.97" red for exit), TradingView-style. Dot sits inside/beside the candle at the exact price; tag offset away from the candle with the connecting line.
Open design questions for the build session:
- Marker size: much smaller than current qty-scaled circles. Fixed small size, or still subtly qty-scaled?
- Leader line: style (solid/thin), color (match entry/exit green/red), length/angle.
- Price tag: format (price to 2-4 dp), bg color by entry/exit, positioning to avoid overlapping candles/each other.
- STACKED FILLS: on coarse timeframes multiple fills snap to one bar (trade 61: 6 fills → 2-3 dots on 5M). Multiple leader lines/tags at one time must not overlap — need a stacking/fanning strategy.
- v5 API: does createSeriesMarkers support leader lines + custom labels, or does this need a custom series primitive / separate price-line+label rendering? (Research during build.)
Belongs to: the existing "leader-line markers" step in the v0.2.4 chart build order (after theme pass). NOT part of the current framing/marker-placement fix.
