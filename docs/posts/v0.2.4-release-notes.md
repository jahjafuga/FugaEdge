This release adds the Technical Analysis tab: six sections that read your indicator state at the moment you entered each trade, for the small-cap momentum stack — MACD, VWAP, and the 9/20 EMA. It answers a question no other journal does: did your entries actually follow the system, and did the discipline pay?

## New: Technical Analysis tab

Six sections, each reading your indicator state at entry on a 1-minute or 5-minute basis:

- **Header Strip** — your discipline score (the % of entries where MACD was positive AND price was above VWAP AND above the 9 EMA) beside three alignment cards, each with that subset's win rate and net P&L.
- **MACD State** — the four-bucket positive/negative × rising/falling grid, built on Ross Cameron's "only trade when the MACD is positive" rule.
- **VWAP distance** — seven signed-distance buckets, from "below / broken" through "at VWAP" to "parabolic."
- **EMA distance** — six 9-EMA distance buckets plus a separate 9/20 stacking strip (stacked vs broken at entry).
- **Combined Signal Reads** — a full-alignment vs any-misalignment comparison: are you trading the whole system, and does it beat breaking it?
- **Time-of-Day** — a 5×4 entry-time × MACD-state matrix over the first-two-hours volatility regimes (pre-9:30 through 12:00+ ET).

Every bucket and cell clicks open to the exact trades behind it, drilling into a read-only trade detail. A designed color palette encodes each bucket's position on the equilibrium axis — green at the sweet spot, red on the broken and parabolic extremes — so "near the level vs extended" reads at a glance.

## On-chart MACD pane

A fourth indicator toggle in the trade-detail chart, alongside 9 EMA / 20 EMA / VWAP. It's a saved, default-on preference: hide the MACD sub-pane and it stays hidden across every trade you open, until you turn it back on.

## Chart improvements

- A redesigned fill-ladder: pill markers with entries on the left and exits on the right, side-colored leaders, and legibility polish (outline, shadow, leader halo) — replacing the old fill dots.
- Fills snapped to the bar whose interval actually contains them.
- Average entry/exit reference lines.
- Warmup-bars-driven indicator compute, so MACD / VWAP / EMA read correctly on the first bars of the session instead of warming up cold.

## Under the hood

- The full multi-session Technical Analysis spec build (Sessions 1–8).
- A reusable component foundation — accordion, sortable trade table, bucket cards/rows, diverging-bar primitive — extracted from the first section so each later section composes rather than re-implements.
- The test suite grew from 1,014 to 1,290 (+276) across the v0.2.4 build.
- The aggregation logic lives in pure modules under `src/core/technicals` with zero Electron or database imports — already portable to the planned web version.

## Known limitations

VWAP distance has limited coverage for pre-market-heavy traders: session VWAP doesn't exist before the 9:30 ET regular-session open, so pre-market entries currently can't be classified against it. The tab surfaces this honestly with a "(NO VWAP DATA)" exclusion chip rather than hiding it; proper handling is under investigation for v0.2.5.

## Auto-updater notes

v0.2.3 installs will auto-prompt for v0.2.4. The installer is unsigned (consistent with prior releases), so Windows SmartScreen shows the usual unverified-publisher prompt on a fresh install.
